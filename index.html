about us

At Bs Cafe Ampati, we believe in the rhythm of the seasons. Our kitchen works with local farms to bring you ingredients at their peak — from autumn roots to spring greens.

Inspired by the earthy tones of terracotta and olive groves, our space is designed for unhurried meals and genuine connection. Whether it's a weekday lunch or a celebration, you'll find a menu that changes with nature.
